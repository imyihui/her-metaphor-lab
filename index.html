Her Metaphor Lab

Alternative Narratives of Becoming Not to explain a life but to resist how it is
expected to unfold

Her Metaphor Lab is an experimental publishing practice constructing counter-narratives of growing. We collect fragments through interviews and fieldwork. We translate them into non-linear forms.

'Her' marks a displacement. A voice that is present, but not centered.

Still Becoming — Issue 01 · Introduction

Still Becoming Issue 1 explores identity as an ongoing and unfinished process, particularly within the '90s generation.

The project is grounded in investigative research, including interviews and collected fragments of lived experiences. Rather than forming a unified narrative, these materials are intentionally presented as discontinuous and sometimes contradictory moments.

Through non-linear sequencing, metaphorical imagery, and fragmented conversations, the zine constructs a narrative system where meaning is not fixed but emerges through the reader's navigation.

It aims to create a space for unfinished, resistant, and freely expressed forms of becoming, where identity is not concluded but continuously in flux.
